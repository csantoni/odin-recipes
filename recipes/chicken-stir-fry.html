Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Chicken breast with mushrooms and bell peppers, stir fried and accompanied by a savory sauce.


Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breasts
  • 1 lb white button mushrooms
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/8 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/8 tsp granulated garlic
  • 3 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine
  • Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cut mushrooms and bell pepper into thin slices.
  3. In a small bowl, mix ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and rice wine.
  4. Heat sesame oil in a wok until very hot.
  5. Add bell pepper and stir fry for about 2 minutes then add mushrooms and continue cooking until mushrooms are soft and starting to brown, about 5 minutes more. Remove vegetables from wok and set aside.
  6. Stir fry chicken pieces until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  7. While the chicken is cooking, make a slurry with the cornstarch and a bit of water.
  8. Return veggies to pan and add sauce and slurry. Stir and cook until all ingredients are heated through.
  9. Serve immediately over rice.
